📖 Facility Checker Instructions

  • Enter anaddressorlatitude,longitudein the input field.
  • Select which facilities to show:
    • Broadband Facility (BB)
    • Galactus
    • BTS
  • Optionally set aradius (in meters)for each facility type.
  • ClickSearchto find facilities within the selected radius.
  • Facilities appear on the map with their own icons (BB, Galactus, BTS).
  • Use theClear Mapbutton to remove all current markers.
  • Download your results:
    • CSVfor spreadsheet format
    • KMLfor Google Earth or GIS apps
  • The color-codedlegendindicates utilization percentage levels.
