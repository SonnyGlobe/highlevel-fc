📖 Facility Checker Instructions

  • Enter an address or latitude,longitude in the input field.
  • Select which facilities to show:
    • Broadband Facility (BB)
    • Galactus
    • BTS
  • Optionally set a radius (in meters) for each facility type.
  • Click Search to find facilities within the selected radius.
  • Facilities appear on the map with their own icons (BB, Galactus, BTS).
  • Use the Clear Map button to remove all current markers.
  • Download your results:
    • CSV for spreadsheet format
    • KML for Google Earth or GIS apps
  • The color-coded legend indicates utilization percentage levels.
